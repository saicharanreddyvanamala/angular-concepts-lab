Append Pipe Examples

Parameterizing Pipe Example (Truncate)

Default: {{'Angular is awesome and powerful' | truncate}}

Custom Limit: {{'Angular is awesome and powerful' | truncate:{limit: 10} }}

Custom Limit + suffix: {{'Angular is awesome and powerful' | truncate:{limit: 12, suffix: '***'} }}

Custom suffix: {{'Angular is awesome and powerful' | truncate:{suffix: '@@@'} }}

Chaining Pipes Exapmple

original: {{'Sai Charan Reddy'}}

Uppercase + Slice: {{'Sai Charan Reddy' | uppercase | slice:0:3 }}

Truncate + Uppercase: {{'Angular is awesome and powerful' | truncate:{limit: 15, suffix:'$$$'} | uppercase }}

Date Formatting Chain: {{today | date:'medium' | lowercase}}
